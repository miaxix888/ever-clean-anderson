Service Scope

Ever Clean Anderson provides touchless exterior washing services. Some services may be seasonal or limited.

Vehicle Condition

Heavy contamination (mud, tar, sap, cement dust, overspray, etc.) may require additional time or may not be fully removable with a touchless wash. We will communicate any limitations before starting when possible.

Damage & Pre-Existing Issues

We are not responsible for pre-existing paint damage, peeling clear coat, oxidation, cracked trim, loose emblems, or prior repairs that may be affected by normal washing.

Access & Safety

Customer must provide safe access to the vehicle and a suitable work area. Service may be rescheduled due to unsafe conditions, severe weather, or restricted access.

Payments & Deposits

Deposits (if required) reserve an appointment time. No-shows or late cancellations may forfeit the deposit.

Appointment Changes

Please call/text as soon as possible if you need to reschedule. We’ll do our best to accommodate.
